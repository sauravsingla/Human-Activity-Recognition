# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We are committed to making participation in this research-software project respectful, inclusive, and free from harassment.

## Expected behaviour

Contributors should:

- communicate respectfully and focus criticism on technical work;
- document assumptions, limitations, and experimental evidence honestly;
- avoid overstating reproduced results or paper fidelity;
- respect privacy, intellectual property, dataset terms, and software licences;
- accept constructive review and correct mistakes transparently.

## Unacceptable behaviour

Harassment, discrimination, personal attacks, deliberate misinformation, plagiarism, disclosure of private information, and knowingly fabricated experimental results are unacceptable.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately to the repository owner through the GitHub profile linked from this project. Reports will be reviewed promptly and handled as confidentially as reasonably possible. Maintainers may edit or remove contributions, restrict participation, or take other proportionate action when this policy is violated.

## Scope

This policy applies in repository discussions, issues, pull requests, reviews, and other project-related communication.
